# DISCLAIMER

This repository contains the `stock-research` Claude Code skill (and its companion
Codex manifest). The skill produces structured research bundles for any stock
ticker a user requests.

## Not Investment Advice

The output of this skill is **research material only**. It is:

- Generated from public sources retrieved at the documented retrieval date.
- Subject to recency budgets and tier classifications that may exclude or
  downgrade evidence the user expects.
- Capable of being wrong, incomplete, or stale. Markets move; data revisions,
  restatements, and breaking news invalidate outputs continuously.
- Not a recommendation to buy, sell, or hold any security, and not a
  solicitation of any kind.

## No Fiduciary Relationship

Use of this skill does not create a fiduciary, advisory, broker-dealer, or
investment-adviser relationship between the user and any contributor, author,
publisher, or distributor of this skill.

## User Responsibility

Before acting on any information in a research bundle:

1. Verify the cited primary sources directly at the URLs in the bundle.
2. Consult a licensed financial professional registered in your jurisdiction.
3. Consider your personal financial situation, risk tolerance, and time horizon.
4. Treat any tier-A disagreement > ±10% surfaced as `conflict: true` as a hard
   signal to seek a second human opinion before any action.
